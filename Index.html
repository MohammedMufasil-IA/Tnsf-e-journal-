About Us

Who We Are

The Tamilnadu Science Forum (TNSF) was established to promote scientific awareness among the people of Tamil Nadu. Our e-journal publishes articles on science, technology, environment, and society in a simple, reader-friendly style.

We believe science belongs to everyone. Our goal is to bridge the gap between research and the common reader through honest and inclusive writing.
